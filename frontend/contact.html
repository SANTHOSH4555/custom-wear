Quick FAQs

What is the minimum custom order quantity?

You can order even 1 piece! Bulk discounts apply for 10+ pieces.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

PNG, SVG, PDF and AI files. Minimum 300 DPI for best results.

Do you support returns?

Plain items: 7-day return. Custom printed items are non-returnable unless there's a print defect.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, SuperMoney) via QR. Bank transfer for bulk orders.
